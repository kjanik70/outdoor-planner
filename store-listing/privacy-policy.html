Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Overview

Outdoor Planner ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Data Storage

All data you enter into the App — saved locations and weather preferences — is stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to our servers because we do not operate any servers.

Network Requests

The App makes network requests to the following third-party services to provide weather and location search functionality:

  • Open-Meteo Weather API (api.open-meteo.com) — to fetch hourly weather forecasts. Only latitude and longitude coordinates of your saved locations are sent.
  • Open-Meteo Geocoding API (geocoding-api.open-meteo.com) — to search for locations by name. Only the search text you type is sent.

These requests do not include any personal identifiers. Open-Meteo is a free, open-source weather API. Please refer to Open-Meteo's terms for their data practices.

Data Collection

We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Location data from your device's GPS
  • Any form of tracking or advertising data

Third-Party Services

The App does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
